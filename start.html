30 Days of Web Development will provide you with an opportunity to kick start your journey in web development and get hands-on experience with HTML, CSS, JS, Bootstrap, and some basics of back-end. You will also work on some quizzes based on what you have learned along with some projects which will help you get better with the basics each day.
